ABOUT

Jessica del Rosario // UX Researcher and Designer

Hi! I'm Jess, a Behavioural Neuroscience student at UBC interested in full time UX/UI Design & Research roles. I have always had a passion for understanding human cognition/behaviour and using that understanding to help others in need. Along with a love for visual design across all mediums, I found the perfect integration of my two passions in UX research and design. I’m a person who thrives in creative environments and sees each challenge as an opportunity to learn.

When I'm not thinking of simple and creative solutions to interesting problems, I enjoy walking dogs, playing music, running to the beach, and staying involved in mental health advocacy. If you have any questions, feedback, or just want to get to know me more, feel free to shoot me an email —I'd be happy to hear from you :)
