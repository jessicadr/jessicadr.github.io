ABOUT

Hello! I'm Jessica.

I'm Jessica, a neuroscience student and UX/UI designer currently building a design system from scratch at UBC Faculty of Applied Science.

I strive to create intuitive yet elegant design solutions for problems that will improve lives. I am a well-rounded designer with a background in psychology, UX research, and a passion for design across all mediums. I love user-centred design, and devoting my time to research to create experiences that humans can enjoy.


In my free time, you can find me playing/producing music, snowboarding, or playing video games. I am actively looking for future design and research opportunities, and would love to get in contact @ jessicalimdr@gmail.com. :)
