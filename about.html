ABOUT

Hello! I'm Jessica.

I'm Jessica, a neuroscience student and UX/UI designer currently building a design system at UBC Faculty of Applied Science, and designing websites with nwPlus.

I strive to create intuitive yet elegant design solutions for problems that will improve lives. With a passion for design across all mediums and an academic background in research, I found the perfect integration of my skills in UX design. I am a person who is highly devoted to human-centered design, thrives in creative environments, and sees each challenge as an opportunity to learn.


You can often find me snowboarding, playing music, drawing, or playing video games. I am actively looking for future internship opportunities, and would love to get in contact @ jessicalimdr@gmail.com. :)
