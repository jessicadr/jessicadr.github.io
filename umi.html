UBC Music Initiative

How might we redesign a website so that it aligns with a club's updated branding and message?

I am currently completing the design for this project, feel free to check out what we have so far:

PROJECT DETAILS

Product: Redesigned Website
Team: Jessica del Rosario, Ervin Wong, Daniel Decolongon
Role: Webmaster


UBC Music Initiative (UMI) is a student-led club at the University of British Columbia that focuses on outreach, school events, and community-building through music.

GOAL

Redesign UMI's website to fit the recent rebranding - which involved a change in logo and colour scheme - along with ensuring that the main message of the club is accurately conveyed.

RESEARCH

Process:

I worked closely with UMI's Marketing Director and Graphic Designer to understand the message they would like to convey, and identify the pain points within the website which still followed the older branding.

5 Pain Points:

  1. Empty pages: pages such as the Members' Reflections page had little to no content and served no functional purpose.
  2. Text-heavy: paragraphs of text without images created crowded interface.
  3. Lack of brand presence: black and white colour scheme did not accurately fit the rebranding of the club.
  4. Misrepresentative message: updated message focuses on club events and community-building, while old message stuck to a professional and classic theme.
  5. Outdated interface: The use of serif fonts created an obsolete interface and further crowded text-heavy pages.

LIMITATIONS

  • I was asked to redesign the website within a Squarespace template in order to allow future club executives easier access to add events to website.
  • Time constraint of 2 weeks as Marketing Director wanted to launch the page before the next school term began.

SOLUTIONS

  • Remove unnecessary pages from navigation bar, leaving only: About, Booking & Rentals, Join Us, Contact.
  • Use video from past event as landing page background: dynamically showcase UMI's main purpose, rather than using still images.
  • General interface and branding update: Minimize text, add supporting images, change to sans-serif font, incorporate colour scheme.
  • Include a call to action to motivate students to sign up: "Join Us" button.
