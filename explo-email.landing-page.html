Explo - Email Landing Page

Desiging a custom landing page for Explo email reports.

Project details

Team: Carly Stanisic - Product Designer, Andrew Chen - Cofounder, Jordan Greissman - Software Engineer
Role: Product Designer
Timeframe: 1 week
Tools: Figma
Live website: https://www.explo.co/email-reports

Goal

Design a landing page that informs our customers on the launch of our new email reporting feature, while promoting our product and maintaining branding.

Process

  1. Research best practices for successful landing page design + copy
  2. Find relevant design examples and inspiration
  3. Review previous design examples in our website
  4. Sketching, prototyping, iterating
